      Squad

    • Transfer Market

      • In modern football buying and selling players is the key to maintain a strong squad that wins titles. This feature is to take your FootballManager experience from ordinary to full of engagement and excitement. Each Manager has a bank on which their money is stored, from this bank account purchase of player can be made, also when player has been sold the money goes there.

    • Training

      • The manager may train his/her player individually on a daily basis. Training is a useful feature to improve a player’s ability, yet this may cause fatigue, which oft lead to injuries.

    • Health Statistics

      • To ensure FootballManager maintains a fun and real life aspect to the game, each player on each squad has an indicator which indicates their stamina, fatigue level. Keeping check of each player’s health stats will help the manager prevent injuries and reduce chances of winning a game.

    • Injuries

      • Injuries are a part of every sport. FootballManager has decided to include this vital feature also. If a player’s health statistic is low due to participation in many games and training they will become venerable to injury. This injury occurs for a certain day/week depending on the severity of the injury.
